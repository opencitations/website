OpenCitations Index Search Interface

Here you can perform queries on the OpenCitations Index datasets. Such as:

  • REFERENCES: get the references of a document with DOI: 10.1186/1756-8722-6-59
  • CITATIONS: get the citations of a document with DOI: 10.1186/1756-8722-5-31
  • A CITATION: get the data of a citation with OCI: 06190834283-06101389277
